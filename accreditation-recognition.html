Central Institute of School Education- GOVT. REGISTRATION

The Central Institute of School Education was registered. To offer specialized services to the government and other agencies in the field of education, in general, and school education, in particular, for the development and growth of distance and open school system and develop proper curriculum for vocational and enduring education to prepare student population for the world of work. To provide an academic environment that treats each child as a unique individual and develops him/ her to the maximum potential.

CISE Is An Institute affiliated BSS, established in 1952 by Planning Commission, Govt. of India
