    About cise India

    “INDIAN INSTITUTE OF OPEN SCHOOLING (cise)” is established on this day, 31 October 2013. To accomplish the mission and vision of Father Mr. Rambali & Mother Mrs. Shobhavati Devi, by "ER. DILIP KUMAR" (Founder and Chairman).

    “INDIAN INSTITUTE OF OPEN SCHOOLING (cise)” is AN ISO 9001: 2015 certified Organization. “INDIAN INSTITUTE OF OPEN SCHOOLING (cise)” is run BY All India Institute of Training and Education - Regd. By Govt. of NCT, Delhi (INDIA). Under the registration act ITA - 1882 by Govt. of India.
